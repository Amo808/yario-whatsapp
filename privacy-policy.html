Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 WhatsApp Business Data

When you connect your WhatsApp Business account to our platform, we collect and process:

  • WhatsApp messages sent to your business number
  • Customer contact information (phone numbers, profile names)
  • Message content, including text, images, and documents
  • Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status)
  • WhatsApp Business profile information

1.2 Account Information

We collect information about your business account:

  • Business name and contact details
  • Manager and staff information
  • Telegram chat IDs for notifications
  • API tokens and authentication credentials
  • Account settings and preferences

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our service:

  • Ticket creation and management activities
  • Manager assignments and response times
  • System interactions and API calls
  • Error logs and performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Functionality

  • Automated Ticket Creation: Process WhatsApp messages to create support tickets
  • AI Analysis: Categorize messages using artificial intelligence
  • Manager Assignment: Automatically route tickets to appropriate support staff
  • Notifications: Send alerts to managers via Telegram
  • Response Management: Facilitate responses to customer inquiries

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our algorithms
  • Debug technical issues and improve system performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Generate anonymized analytics and insights

2.3 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders
  • Protect our rights and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

  • WhatsApp Business API: Message processing through Meta platforms
  • Telegram API: Notification delivery to managers
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Secure hosting and data processing
  • AI Services: Message analysis and categorization

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization mechanisms
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
  • Auditing: Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Staff Training: Security awareness training for all personnel

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Closed Accounts: Data retained for 30 days after account closure
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@yario.ai

7. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
