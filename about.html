About Yario

Transforming Customer Communication with AI-Powered Automation

Our Company

Yario is a technology company specializing in WhatsApp Business automation solutions. Founded in 2024, we help businesses streamline their customer communication through intelligent automation, AI-powered message analysis, and seamless integration with popular business tools.

Our platform is trusted by property management companies, hotels, and service businesses across multiple countries to handle customer inquiries, manage support tickets, and improve response times.

What We Do

WhatsApp Automation

Automated message processing, intelligent routing, and instant responses to customer inquiries.

AI-Powered Analysis

Advanced AI categorizes messages and extracts key information automatically.

Ticket Management

Convert conversations into organized support tickets with priority handling.

Team Notifications

Real-time alerts to managers via Telegram for quick response times.

Industries We Serve

  • Hotels & Resorts: Guest services, room bookings, concierge requests
  • Property Management: Tenant inquiries, maintenance requests, booking management
  • Retail & E-commerce: Order tracking, customer support, returns processing
  • Service Businesses: Appointment scheduling, service inquiries, support tickets
