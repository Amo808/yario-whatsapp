Data Deletion Request

Request deletion of your personal data from Yario WhatsApp Business Automation

How to Request Data Deletion

In accordance with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. You can submit a data deletion request using the form below or by contacting us directly.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • WhatsApp messages and conversation history
  • Customer contact information (phone numbers, profile names)
  • Support tickets and related data
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Manager assignments and notification history
  • Analytics and usage data

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Data Retention Notice

Please note that some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes:

  • Financial transaction records (retained for 7 years for tax purposes)
  • Legal compliance records (retained as required by law)
  • Anonymized analytics data (may be retained indefinitely)
